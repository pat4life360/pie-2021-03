Project Mission

Our goal as a team was to create a remote-controlled car with the ability to shoot objects on command. Our car is utilizing omnidirectional wheels for increased maneuverability. It also features a camera allowing the user to see when controlling through a small LCD screen.

Inspiration

The inspiration of this project, as previously mentioned, is from the widely popular first-person shooter Rainbow Six Siege.

About Rainbow Six: Siege

Is a tactical shooter in which players utilize a plethora of remote controlled mini reconnaissance robots.

Twitch Drone

This is the inspiration for our project. This drone can be deployed by players to scout rooms and shoot projectiles.

Outcome

We created a small remote-controlled vehicle with omnidirectional wheels for mobility that was able to fire a projectile at significant speeds
