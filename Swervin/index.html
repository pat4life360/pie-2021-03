Intro

Meet Swervo, our proof of concept of a rideable holonomic mobility platform. Each wheel on Swervo can spin around the horizontal axis while pivoting around the vertical axis, allowing for hyper maneuverability and a omnidirectional range of motion. Imagine having an electric skateboard, but with maximum swerve.
